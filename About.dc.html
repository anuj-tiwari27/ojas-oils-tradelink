OUR STORY

Built on relationships, run on trust.

Since 1993, Ojas Oils & Tradelink has worked at the heart of the edible oil trade — connecting solvent plants, refiners, mills, and bulk buyers across the country. We started as a specialist brokerage in soybean oil and have grown alongside the market, adding mustard seed, cake, meal, and traditional ghani oil to the desk.

Through every price cycle and season, our promise has stayed the same: honest counsel, transparent pricing, and deals that settle cleanly. That consistency is why the same mills and buyers have traded through us for decades.
